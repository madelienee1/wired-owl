WHO ARE WE?

The Wired Owl Coffee Co. has been serving coffee to the Shorncliffe and surrounds community for over 6 years. We serve a custom blend called 'Two Hoots' from local roastery, Wolff Coffee Roasters, and offer a range of products from local suppliers. We are a dog friendly cafe, your furry friends are more than welcome! If you're short on time, order on Bopple or text ahead to 0413 849 814
