WHO ARE WE?

The Wired Owl Coffee Co. has been serving coffee to the Shorncliffe and surrounds community for over 6 years. We proudly serve local roastery, Wolff Coffee Roasters, and offer a range of sweet and savoury treats from local suppliers. We are a dog friendly cafe, your furry friends are more than welcome! Our sunny little corner in Shorncliffe is the perfect spot for your morning coffee, a catch up with friends or perhaps some time to yourself. If you're short on time, order on Bopple or text ahead to 0413 849 814.

SUPPORTING LOCAL

All of our products are sourced from other local businesses from Brisbane to the Sunshine Coast. We pride ourselves on our tightknit community and supporting other small businesses.
