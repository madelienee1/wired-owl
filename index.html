WHO ARE WE?

The Wired Owl Coffee Co. has been serving coffee to the Shorncliffe and surrounds community for over 6 years. We proudly serve local roastery, Wolff Coffee Roasters, and offer a range of sweet and savoury treats from local suppliers. We are a dog friendly cafe, your furry friends are more than welcome! The perfect spot for your morning coffee, a catch up with friends or perhaps some time to yourself. If you're short on time, order on Bopple or text ahead 0413 849 814.

SUPPORTING LOCAL

All of our products are sourced from local family owned businesses. We pride ourselves on community and supporting other small local businesses.
